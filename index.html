About me

My parents gave me my first camera when i was 12. What started out as a hobby has grown into a lifelong love affair with photography.
I am a student in one of the best London photography schools. I speak English, Latvian and Russian fluently.
My goal is to show the beauty, grace and strength of people. I focus on non-models and place regular people in exaggerated situations that highlight an aspect of who they are.
